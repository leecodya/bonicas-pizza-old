Kitchen Assistant

Bonica's Pizza has an immediate opening for a part-time kitchen assistant at both of our locations. The successful candidate will be responsible for maintaining a clean work environment by tending to dishes that may be left on tables or in the kitchen that need the proper upkeep. This position will also be responsible for safely, efficiently, and courteously serving customers.

This is a part-time position working approximately 20 hours per week with occasional work on weekends.

SCOPE:

Incumbents assure the kitchen and dining aread are well cared for and clean This is a self-directed position and the person in this position must be well-organized and committed to a high standard of work. This is a physical job and requires lifting of 15 or more pounds, bending, twisting, etc.

Ideal candidate will:

  • Be able to work in a fast paced, team environment.
  • Be able to offer service with a good attitude
  • Be able to know proper cleaning methods for optimal sanitation

KEY DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:

Key duties included but are not limited to the following:

  1. Adhere to company high quality standards and customer service.
  2. Responsible for the condition of equipment used and for reporting specific problems as necessary.
  3. Strive to maintain safety standards.

REQUIREMENTS:

  • Perform Physically Demanding Activities
  • Satisfactory Job Record
  • Good physical condition
  • Drug Free Workplace
  • Able to work a flexible schedule, with possibiity of some weekend work
